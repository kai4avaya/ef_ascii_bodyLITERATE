Executive Functions Coaching & Mindfulness

bodyLITERATE is a team of learning and movement specialists. We combine one-on-one executive functions coaching with mindfulness and somatic modalities. We work alongside families and schools to support neurodiverse learners. Founder and director, Kai Kleinbard, has over a decade of experience helping young learners thrive.

Two questions guide our work

Lens 1: External Challenges

How do we help learners approach external challenges? We work with learners to develop better work habits. We help them build executive function skills through one-on-one support.

Lens 2: Internal Skills

How do we help learners develop internal skills to manage emotions and focus better? We work with learners to develop a toolkit of mindfulness skills. Students become more aware of their focus and attention. They learn how to better navigate stress and difficult emotions.

Putting It Together

Our one-on-one executive function coaching combines both lenses. Students learn life-long executive function skills to manage work and routines. They learn how to navigate stress and anxiety. We take a developmental approach, tailoring both lenses to support joyful, holistic learning.

Our Process

We offer one-on-one coaching specifically designed to enhance and strengthen your executive function skills. These include planning, attention management, emotional regulation, and problem-solving, complemented by mindfulness strategies.
